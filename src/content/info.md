## About This Site

Welcome to **RikiTraki**, a place to explore and share GPS tracks.

Use the interactive map to browse outdoor experiences and learn more about their locations.  
Tracks, markers, and photos are clickable.

The map includes:

- zoom controls  
- base map and layer selectors  
- an elevation profile widget  
- a track info panel with slideshow (when photos are available)

You’re welcome to register if you’d like to upload and share your own tracks.

**RikiTraki is a work in progress (currently in Beta).**  
Bug reports and suggestions are always appreciated.

To learn more about this and other projects, visit the blog:  
<https://morinricardo.com>
